About this Project

BrainScale CRM is a multi-tenant CRM for managing students and customers, call workflows, follow-ups, and team collaboration. It was designed to handle real operational constraints — multi-tenancy, role-based access, and background job processing — without sacrificing maintainability. The system design prioritizes long-term ownership over short-term delivery speed.
